Design and Build Project | September–December 2022

Electric Skateboard

A wooden electric skateboard designed and assembled as a compact personal-transport project.

The project combined woodworking, mechanical assembly and integration of an electric drivetrain. The deck geometry was developed to provide sufficient standing area while leaving space for the trucks, wheels and electrical hardware beneath the board.

  • Woodworking
  • Mechanical Assembly
  • Electric Drive
  • Product Design

01

Design and Construction

Balancing structural strength, rider space and drivetrain packaging.

Wooden deck

I shaped and finished the wooden deck to create a stable riding platform with clear mounting regions for the front and rear trucks. The profile was designed to balance usable standing space with a compact overall form.

System integration

The electric drive, trucks, wheels and controls were assembled around the deck. Component placement had to preserve ground clearance, avoid interference with the wheels and keep the board balanced during use.

02

Outcome

The completed build gave me early experience turning a mechanical concept into a functioning physical system. It developed my practical understanding of manufacturing, component integration and the relationship between structural form and user experience.

About

I am a Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics student at the University of Pennsylvania with experience taking products from early user research through CAD development, modelling, manufacturing and prototype testing.

My projects have developed my experience in SolidWorks, additive manufacturing, material selection, Python, Arduino electronics and engineering validation. I particularly enjoy projects that combine physical systems with computational modelling or automated control.

Outside the classroom, I am a coxswain on Penn’s Varsity Heavyweight Rowing Team. This role has strengthened my leadership, communication and decision-making in a demanding team environment.
